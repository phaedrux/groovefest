Spring Groovefest 2007 was held on Earth Day (Sunday, April 22, 2007) at Andrews Park in Norman, Oklahoma.
The lineup included: Charlie Rayl Students, Proprietors of the Earth, Kasra George Ahmadi Jazz,
Aalim Dance Academy, Joe Mack & Travis Linville, Galapagos, Reido, and the Tall Cotton String Band.
