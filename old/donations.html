Donations

Human Rights are the Heart & Soul of Groovefest. Please bring donations of non-perishable food & person hygiene items for adults, kids, & babies to help those in need in our community.

If you light a lamp for somebody, it will also brighten your path.
-Buddhist Proverb

Groovefest, in cooperation with the Norman Unitarian Universalist Fellowship (NUUF), will distribute your generous donations to Norman non-profit agencies serving those in need in our community. Donations can be dropped off at the NUUF booth during Groovefest.

Suggested Donations

Non-perishable Food: Beans & rice, assorted varieties; Cold & Hot cereal; Pasta & sauce; PB & J, apple butter, honey; Tuna, canned chicken & turkey, Canned soup & crackers; Hamburger & Tuna Helper, other boxed dinners; Canned vegetables & fruit; Fruit juice & juice boxes; Applesauce & fruit sauces; Flour, corn meal, corn bread mix Canola & olive oil

Personal Care Iems: Soap, body wash, shampoo & hair care products, African-American hair products, feminine hygiene items, toothbrushes & toothpaste, floss, deodorant, body lotion, nail clippers & files

Baby Items: Diapers (Sizes 3-6), wipes, baby oil, lotion, powder, diaper rash ointment, nasal synergies, teething rings

New & Used, Good Condition Please, Winter Clothing Items: Coats, hats, gloves, scarves, heavy socks, sweaters, sweatshirts, thermal underwear, boots, blankets, sleeping bags, hand warmer packets. This is a great chance to clear out your closet and round up things the kids have outgrown.
