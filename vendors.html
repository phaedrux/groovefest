Vendor Registration

We hope that this year will be the biggest and best Groovefest yet, and to do that, we need participation from the wonderful Norman community. Specifically, we need the support of vendors to make the event possible.

Click here for more information about becoming a Sponsor, Vendor or Volunteer! 

Vendors and sponsors make sucess possible for Groovefest to support local community businesses, arts, music, and human rights awareness. Information booths about local organizations are welcome too. Please contact us at groovefest@gmail.com
