32nd Anniversary Groovefest - October 7th, 2018

8 bands, performers, speakers, vendors, arts, community, Human Rights for all!

The Groovefest mission is to raise human rights awareness through arts, music and activist speakers from local organizations who inspire us to honor individual freedom and do what we can in our own lives or communities to change things for the better - and to remember the interwoven web of Human Rights that connects all people fighting injustice worldwide. Norman Groovefest has been organizing a free music festival to promote Human Rights awareness since 1986. We are currently the only Human Rights music festival in Oklahoma, and the 2nd longest-running Human Rights music festival in the world.
